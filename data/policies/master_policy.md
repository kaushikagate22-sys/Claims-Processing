# Claims Processing Rulebook

Validation guidelines for five claim types. The agent classifies the claim,
then applies the type's mandatory documents, validation rules, and decision
logic. Outcomes: Approve, Hold, Escalate, Reject.

## WARRANTY CLAIM
Repair/replacement of failed components under warranty.
- Mandatory: warranty claim form, machine serial, hour meter, job card, technician failure code, failed part, parts/labour invoice.
- Reject: expired/out-of-warranty, misuse, accident, external damage, wear and tear, non-covered part, duplicate.
- Hold: missing mandatory document/photo, inconsistent diagnosis, price/hour mismatch.
- Escalate: repeat failure, high value, late claim, safety issue, ambiguity.
- Approve: all eligibility, document, technical and amount checks pass.

## NEW EQUIPMENT PRE-INSPECTION (NEPI) CLAIM
Issues found before customer handover of newly dispatched equipment.
- Mandatory: NEPI form, pre-delivery checklist, dispatch note, packing list, machine/defect photos, hour meter.
- Reject: issue after handover/use, not part of standard supply, yard handling, duplicate with transit/insurance.
- Hold: missing checklist, incomplete photos, unclear handover status.
- Escalate: high-value/structural damage, safety defect, repeated missing items, delivery risk.
- Approve: pre-handover issue proven with dispatch/inspection evidence and reasonable cost.

## PARTS REPLACEMENT CLAIM
Replacement of defective spare parts under part warranty.
- Mandatory: claim form, purchase invoice, part/batch/serial number, failure description, technician report, RMA/return note.
- Reject: expired part warranty, non-genuine part, wrong application, fitment error, duplicate, missing proof of failure.
- Hold: missing invoice, missing serial/batch, failed part not returned, unclear fitment.
- Escalate: repeat failure, batch issue, high-cost assembly, safety-critical part, supplier defect.
- Approve: genuine part within warranty, failure proven, return/RMA complete, cost within policy.

## TRANSIT DAMAGE CLAIM
Damage during transportation to dealer yard or customer site.
- Mandatory: claim form, LR/consignment note, delivery challan, vehicle/transporter details, receipt remarks, damage photos.
- Reject: damage after receipt, no transit evidence, yard mishandling, duplicate insurance settlement.
- Hold: no LR remarks, no transporter confirmation, unclear timing, incomplete photos.
- Escalate: major structural damage, customer rejection, safety risk, disputed liability, high value.
- Approve: damage at/before receipt with immediate evidence, clear liability, justified cost.

## EMPLOYEE REIMBURSEMENT CLAIM
Field expenses claimed by service employees.
- Mandatory: claim form, manager approval, visit/job linkage, fuel/toll/hotel/meal receipts.
- Reject: personal expense, duplicate bill, no business purpose, outside approved period, non-compliant mode.
- Hold: missing receipt/approval, unclear job linkage, amount exceeds entitlement.
- Escalate: policy exception, high-value claim, repeated late submissions, suspected duplicate.
- Approve: employee, purpose, dates, bills, limits and approvals valid.
